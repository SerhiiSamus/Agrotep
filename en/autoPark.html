Modern and innovative vehicle fleet

Our fleet consists of more than 250 state-of-the-art Euro 6 DAF and Mercedec tractors manufactured to the highest quality and environmental leadership standards

We grow with you, regularly renewing our fleet, keeping pace with the times and modern technologies in the automotive industry

GPS-monitoring

Agrotep's vehicles are equipped with a satellite tracking system, which allows for continuous monitoring of the road train's movement. We provide our regular customers with personalized access to track transportation online

Automotive fuel

To preserve the environment, we highly value high-quality fuel for our vehicles. We use only the best fuel from producers in Ukraine and Europe. Our contribution to environmental protection was highly appreciated by the Federation of Employers of Ukraine in the nomination “For Environmental Responsibility”

Technical condition of the vehicles

We have many years of experience in servicing trucks. Our vehicles undergo a scheduled technical inspection after each transportation and are serviced in accordance with international standards

Temperature control

All vehicles are equipped with thermographs with printouts. The semi-trailers have sanitary passports and FRC certificates for safe temperature-controlled cargo transportation

Our trucks are equipped with

  • Refrigerated semi-trailers from Schmitz Cargobull - the leader in technology and quality of European trucks
  • Bi-Temp refrigerated trucks with dual temperature conditions
  • Doubledecker refrigerators with two-level loading for 66 pallets
  • Tilt semi-trailers with a volume of up to 95 cubic meters
  • Semi-trailers for transportation of textile products
