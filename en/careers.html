International transportation driver

Ukrainian transport company AGROTEP invites candidates for the position of CE category drivers. The main requirements for applicants include having a CE category driver's license and a responsible attitude towards their work duties. The primary tasks of the drivers will be to transport cargo on routes between Ukraine and Europe or exclusively within Ukraine.

Repair service

We invite candidates to join our repair service team. The main requirements for candidates include professional skills in the repair and maintenance of vehicles, as well as a responsible attitude towards their work tasks. The primary responsibilities of the employees will be diagnostics, repair, and maintenance of vehicles.
