Our fleet

The main characteristics of our fleet, which includes over 250 trucks, are quality, environmental sustainability, and innovation. With 99% of our vehicle combinations meeting EURO-6 standards, we are able to deliver exceptional service at the highest levels in Europe and worldwide. As one of the leading logistics companies in Ukraine, we operate only the most advanced vehicle models, including DAF and Mercedes-Benz. Our use of new Schmitz semitrailers ensures maximum cargo preservation during all types of transportation.

Agrotep Training Class

"AGROTEP" LTD provides quality and professional driver training in its own training class. The training is conducted according to approved programs, including the "Code 95" program. The class meets high standards of quality and safety, ensuring training at the highest level. The instructors have the appropriate qualifications and extensive experience, providing drivers with both theoretical and practical knowledge and skills. We implement innovative teaching methods and use modern equipment, guaranteeing the highest quality and effectiveness of training.

Providing Maintenance and Repair for Vehicles

Our repair and maintenance station is designed and equipped according to international standards. Our specialists provide timely and quality repair and maintenance for both our fleet and the vehicles of our partner clients. The service station is equipped with the necessary tools for warranty service of various vehicle brands.

The service station has 12 repair fields, where the following services are provided:

  • Diagnostic services
  • Welding services
  • Bodywork
  • Tire fitting
  • Truck washing
