Your own training classroom

At Agrotep, we are proud of our own 95-code training room, which is essential for preparing drivers for the professional performance of their work in the transportation sector. Our training room meets the highest quality and safety standards, which ensures that our drivers receive training at the highest level. Our training classroom is staffed only by qualified and experienced instructors who have extensive practical experience in teaching. They use their own innovative teaching methods and modern equipment to ensure maximum efficiency and quality of the learning process.

Own vehicle maintenance and repair service

The company's own repair and maintenance station is designed and equipped in accordance with European standards, and its specialists ensure timely and high-quality maintenance of both its own fleet and the vehicles of its partner clients. The service station has 12 repair bays: diagnostic, welding, painting, alignment, tire fitting and automatic truck wash bays. The service station has all the necessary equipment for warranty service of various brands.
